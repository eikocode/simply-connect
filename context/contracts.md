IP Assignment Clause (from EIPA-2026-006-HK, Section 3):
Employee irrevocably assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in Work Product including:
- All global patent rights, copyright, trade secret rights, mask work rights, and moral rights (to extent waivable)
- Right to file, prosecute, maintain, and enforce patent applications
- All future rights, extensions, renewals, and divisions
Work Product deemed 'work made for hire' to fullest extent permitted by law. Fallback assignment covers anything not qualifying as work made for hire.
